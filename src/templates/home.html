Welcome

Welcome to the Speed ACM Minecraft Website. Our group is made up almost entirely of students at the University of Louisville Speed School of Engineering, and we are almost all members of the ACM. We use this site as a base for our minecraft-related projects. We currently operate one minecraft alpha server, but we intend to have more in the near future. We keep all our server statistics here and keep an updated map, with some really cool zoom functionality.

Most of us are also open source advocates, and we take pride in the software we have chosen. In fact, we have even chosen to make this project open source. We keep an updated list of all the open source software we use here.
